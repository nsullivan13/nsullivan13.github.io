SPHS Music

This is a rebrand for the Severna Park High School Music program. The client was looking for a new logo that included variations for the Music program's different groups, including the band, orchestra, marching band, color guard and jazz band.
The falcon is the original mascot/logo for the school, and was used upon client's request to maintain consistency with the branding for the school's other programs.
