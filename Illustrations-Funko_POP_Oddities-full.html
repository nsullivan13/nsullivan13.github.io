Funko POP! 'Oddities'

This is an ongoing project of digital Funko POP!-styled avatars for the 'Oddities' from the movie The Greatest Showman. Through social media, most of these designs have gained recognition and praise from the actors and actresses who portrayed the oddities in the film.
Click on an oddity to view reference pictures of each character and to read the actors' and actresses' responses.
